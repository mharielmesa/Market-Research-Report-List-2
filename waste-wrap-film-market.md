<p><p>Waste wrap film market includes two main types: Shrink Film and Stretch Film. Shrink film is a type of packaging material that shrinks when heat is applied, tightly conforming to the shape of the product being wrapped. Stretch film, on the other hand, is a stretchable plastic film commonly used to secure and protect items for storage or transportation. Both types are widely used in industries such as food packaging, logistics, and retail for wrapping, securing, and protecting products.</p></p>
