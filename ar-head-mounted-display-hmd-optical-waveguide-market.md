<p><ul><li>Geometric Waveguide</li><li>Diffractive Waveguide (Surface Relief Grating)</li><li>Diffractive Waveguide (Volume Holographic Gratings)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AR Head Mounted Display (HMD) Optical Waveguide market includes three main types: Geometric Waveguide, Diffractive Waveguide (Surface Relief Grating), and Diffractive Waveguide (Volume Holographic Gratings). Geometric waveguides use total internal reflection to guide light, while diffractive waveguides manipulate light using diffraction gratings on the surface or within the volume of the waveguide. Each type offers unique benefits and challenges in terms of size, weight, field of view, and image quality for AR applications.</p></p>
