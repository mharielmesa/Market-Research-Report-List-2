<p><p>Acrylic thermoplastic films can be categorized into two main types: soft type and hard type. Soft type films are flexible and conform easily to curved surfaces, making them suitable for applications where flexibility is important. On the other hand, hard type films are rigid and provide excellent protection against scratches and impact. They are commonly used in high-stress applications where durability is required. Both types offer excellent clarity, weather resistance, and chemical resistance, making them versatile options for a wide range of industries.</p></p>
