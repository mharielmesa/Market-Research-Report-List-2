<p><p>Medical Grade EMI Filter Market consists of two main types: Single Phase EMI Filters and Three Phase EMI Filters. Single Phase EMI Filters are designed to suppress electromagnetic interference in single-phase power lines, commonly used in medical equipment like MRI machines. Three Phase EMI Filters serve the same purpose but are specifically designed for three-phase power systems, found in larger medical devices and equipment. Both types of filters play a crucial role in maintaining the safety and reliability of medical equipment by reducing electromagnetic interference.</p></p>
