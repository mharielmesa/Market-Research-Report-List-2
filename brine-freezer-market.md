<p><p>Brine freezer market types include immersion type, continuous immersion type, and continuous shower type. The immersion type involves submerging the product in a brine solution for freezing. The continuous immersion type allows products to move continuously through a brine solution. The continuous shower type uses a shower system to continuously spray the product with brine as it moves along a conveyor belt. Each type offers unique advantages for different freezing needs and production processes in the food industry.</p></p>
