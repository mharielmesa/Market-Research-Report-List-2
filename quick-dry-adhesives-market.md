<p><p>Quick Dry Adhesives market is divided into two main types: Cyanoacrylate and Epoxy-based adhesives. Cyanoacrylate adhesives, also known as super glue, are known for their quick drying time and strong bond on a variety of materials like plastics, rubber, and metal. On the other hand, Epoxy-based adhesives are highly versatile and offer a strong bond on different surfaces such as wood, ceramics, and glass. Both types are popular choices in the market due to their fast curing times and reliability in bonding different materials.</p></p>
