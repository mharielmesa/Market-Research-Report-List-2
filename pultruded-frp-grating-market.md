<p><p>Pultruded FRP grating is a type of Fiberglass Reinforced Plastic grating that is manufactured using a pultrusion process, which involves pulling continuous fibers through a resin bath and then through a heated die. This process results in a strong and durable grating product that is resistant to corrosion, lightweight, and low maintenance.</p><p>The Pultruded FRP Grating Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.2% during the forecast period. The market growth is being driven by the increasing demand for lightweight and corrosion-resistant grating materials in various industries such as construction, oil & gas, and water treatment. Pultruded FRP grating is increasingly being used in applications where traditional materials like steel or aluminum may not be suitable due to their susceptibility to corrosion.</p><p>The latest trends in the Pultruded FRP Grating Market include the development of advanced resins and fiber reinforcements to improve the mechanical properties of the grating, as well as the introduction of new product designs to cater to specific applications.
