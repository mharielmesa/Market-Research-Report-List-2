<p><ul><li>Overhead Transmission</li><li>Underground Transmission</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric power transmission and distribution equipment market consists of two main types: overhead transmission and underground transmission. Overhead transmission involves the use of power lines suspended above ground level to transmit electricity over long distances. This method is cost-effective and relatively easy to maintain. On the other hand, underground transmission involves burying power lines underground, providing a more aesthetically pleasing and reliable option. However, this method is more expensive and harder to repair in case of faults. Both types play a crucial role in delivering electricity to end consumers.</p></p>
