<p><ul><li>Slow Urethane Activator</li><li>Medium Urethane Activator</li><li>Fast Urethane Activator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Urethane activators come in three main types based on their curing speed: slow, medium, and fast. Slow urethane activators have a longer pot life and cure time, making them ideal for projects that require longer working times. Medium urethane activators strike a balance between curing speed and working time, suitable for a wide range of applications. Fast urethane activators, on the other hand, cure quickly and are best for projects that need a rapid turn-around time. Each type caters to different requirements and preferences in the market.</p></p>
