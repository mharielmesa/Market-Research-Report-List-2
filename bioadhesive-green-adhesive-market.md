<p><p>Bioadhesives, also known as green adhesives, are categorized into two main types: plant-based and animal-based. Plant-based bioadhesives are derived from natural sources such as starch, cellulose, and proteins found in plants. These adhesives are biodegradable and environmentally friendly. On the other hand, animal-based bioadhesives are derived from natural sources such as collagen and gelatin obtained from animal tissues. Both plant-based and animal-based bioadhesives offer sustainable alternatives to traditional synthetic adhesives in various industries.</p></p>
