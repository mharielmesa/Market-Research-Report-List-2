<p><p>TVS (Transient Voltage Suppressor) devices are commonly used in the electronics industry to protect sensitive components from voltage spikes. There are two main types of TVS markets: uni-polar and bi-polar. Uni-polar TVS devices are designed to protect against voltage spikes in one direction only, while bi-polar TVS devices can protect against spikes in both directions. Uni-polar TVS devices are often used in applications where protection is only needed in one direction, while bi-polar TVS devices are used in applications where protection is needed in both directions.</p></p>
