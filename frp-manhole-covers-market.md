<p><p>FRP manhole covers are categorized into three main types based on their utility: Gas Manhole Covers, Power Manhole Covers, and Others. Gas manhole covers are specifically designed to withstand the weight and pressure of vehicles passing over them near gas pipelines. Power manhole covers are meant for use near electrical or utility lines and provide protection against potential electrical hazards. The category of Others covers any additional applications, such as telecommunications or water utilities, that do not fall under the gas or power categories.</p></p>
