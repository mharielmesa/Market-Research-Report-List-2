<p><p>Asphalt waterproof coating can be classified into two main types: liquid and dry. Liquid asphalt waterproof coating is a ready-to-use mixture that can be easily applied onto surfaces to provide a protective waterproof barrier. On the other hand, dry asphalt waterproof coating requires mixing with water before application. Both types of coatings offer excellent protection against water infiltration and are commonly used in construction projects such as roofs, foundations, and roads.</p></p>
