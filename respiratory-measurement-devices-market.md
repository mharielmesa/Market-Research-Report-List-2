<p><ul><li>Pulse Oximeters</li><li>Spirometers</li><li>Capnographs</li><li>Peak Flow Meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Respiratory Measurement Devices Market includes Pulse Oximeters, Spirometers, Capnographs, and Peak Flow Meters. Pulse Oximeters measure oxygen saturation levels in the blood. Spirometers assess lung function by measuring air flow and volume. Capnographs monitor the concentration of carbon dioxide in exhaled breath. Peak Flow Meters measure the maximum speed at which a person can exhale air, indicating the severity of conditions like asthma. These devices play a crucial role in diagnosing and managing respiratory conditions, ensuring proper monitoring and treatment.</p></p>
