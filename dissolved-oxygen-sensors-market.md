<p><strong>The Dissolved Oxygen Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical (Luminescent) Type</li><li>Clark Electrochemical (Amperometric) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dissolved oxygen sensors are crucial for monitoring oxygen levels in various industries such as water treatment, aquaculture, and environmental monitoring. The two main types of dissolved oxygen sensors are optical (luminescent) type and Clark electrochemical (amperometric) type. </p><p>Optical sensors measure oxygen levels by detecting the luminescence quenching of a fluorophore in the presence of oxygen, offering high accuracy and fast response times. On the other hand, Clark electrochemical sensors rely on measuring the current generated when oxygen reacts with a sensing electrode, providing continuous and reliable monitoring capabilities.</p></p>
