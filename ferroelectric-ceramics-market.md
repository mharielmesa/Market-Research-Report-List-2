<p><p>Ferroelectric ceramics can be categorized into two main types: solid ferroelectric ceramics and powder ferroelectric ceramics. Solid ferroelectric ceramics are formed by shaping and sintering raw materials into a dense, solid structure. These materials are commonly used in electronic devices due to their stable properties. On the other hand, powder ferroelectric ceramics consist of fine particles that can be processed into various forms such as films, coatings, and composites. These materials are valued for their versatility and ease of processing in a wide range of applications.</p></p>
