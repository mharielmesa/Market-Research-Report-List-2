<p><p>Medium voltage fuses are important components in electrical systems for protecting equipment from overcurrent conditions. There are different types of medium voltage fuses available on the market, including current limiting fuses, expulsion fuses, and others. </p><p>Current limiting fuses are designed to quickly interrupt excessive currents, minimizing damage to the system. Expulsion fuses use a mechanism to create a gas that extinguishes the arc when the fuse blows. Other types of medium voltage fuses include backup fuses, capacitor fuses, and transformer primary fuses.</p></p>
