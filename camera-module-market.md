<p><strong>The Camera Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CMOS</li><li>CCD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The camera module market consists of two main types - CMOS and CCD. CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras are more commonly used due to their low power consumption, high integration capabilities, and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) cameras typically offer higher image quality and better performance in low-light conditions, but tend to be more expensive and consume more power. Both types of cameras have their own strengths and are used in various applications depending on the specific requirements.</p></p>
