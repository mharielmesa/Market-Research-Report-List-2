<p><p>Transient Blocking Units (TBU) are devices designed to protect electronic equipment from voltage surges and transients. There are two main types of TBU markets: Bidirectional TBU, which can handle transients in both directions, and Unidirectional TBU, which only block transients in one direction. Bidirectional TBUs are ideal for applications where transients can occur in both directions, while Unidirectional TBUs are suitable for protecting against transients from a specific source. Both types of TBUs play a crucial role in safeguarding electronic devices from damaging voltage spikes.</p></p>
