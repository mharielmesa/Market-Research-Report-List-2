<p><strong>The Fifth Wheel Couplings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Compensating</li><li>Semi-oscillating</li><li>Fully Oscillating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fifth wheel couplings come in various types to meet different towing needs. Compensating fifth wheel couplings allow limited vertical movement to compensate for uneven terrain. Semi-oscillating fifth wheel couplings have a higher degree of movement in multiple directions, allowing for better maneuverability. Fully oscillating fifth wheel couplings provide the most freedom of movement, allowing for full rotation in multiple directions. Each type offers different levels of flexibility and adaptability, catering to specific requirements of towing vehicles and trailers.</p></p>
