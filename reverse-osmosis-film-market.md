<p><p>Reverse Osmosis Film market comprises of two main types: Cellulose Based and Thin Film Composite. Cellulose Based membranes are made from natural cellulose material and provide good filtration efficiency at a lower cost. On the other hand, Thin Film Composite membranes are made from synthetic materials and offer high performance in terms of rejection rates and durability. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various applications based on specific requirements such as water purification, desalination, and wastewater treatment.</p></p>
