<p><p>The Food & Beverage Plastics market consists of various types of plastics, including PET, HDPE, PVC, LDPE, PP, and PS. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) is commonly used for bottles and packaging due to its lightweight and recyclable nature. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) is known for its strength and durability, often used for containers and jugs. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is versatile and commonly used for packaging and bottles. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) is flexible and used for bags and films. PP (Polypropylene) is heat-resistant and suitable for containers and packaging. PS (Polystyrene) is lightweight and commonly used for foam packaging and cups.</p></p>
