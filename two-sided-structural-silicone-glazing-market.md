<p><p>Two-sided Structural Silicone Glazing market encompasses various types of materials such as aluminum structural framing, glass panels, silicone sealants, and EPDM. Aluminum structural framing provides structural support for the glazing system, while glass panels offer transparency and aesthetic appeal. Silicone sealants are used to bond the glass panels to the framing, providing a secure and weatherproof seal. EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) is a synthetic rubber material commonly used for gaskets and seals in structural glazing applications, offering flexibility and durability.</p></p>
