<p><p>Sample cutters are essential tools used in various industries for cutting different materials into desired shapes and sizes. There are three main types of sample cutters available in the market: Manual, Pneumatic, and Electric. Manual sample cutters are operated by hand, providing precise control over the cutting process. Pneumatic sample cutters use compressed air to power the cutting mechanism, making them suitable for cutting thicker materials. Electric sample cutters are powered by electricity, offering efficient and quick cutting capabilities. Each type of sample cutter caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
