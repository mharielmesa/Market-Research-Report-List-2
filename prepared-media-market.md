<p><p>Prepared media refers to culture media that are ready to use for the growth of microorganisms in laboratories. The market for prepared media is segmented into plated, bottled, tubed, and other types. Plated media are solid media used for culturing bacteria on agar plates. Bottled media are liquid media used for microbial growth in test tubes or bottles. Tubed media are semi-solid media used for culturing bacteria in tubes. Other types include media in various forms like powder, granules, or capsules.</p></p>
