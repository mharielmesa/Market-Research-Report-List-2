<p><p>Suspension concentrates are a type of pesticide formulation that are divided into water suspension and oil suspension markets. Oil suspension concentrates use oil as the solvent for the pesticide particles, providing better penetration and longevity. Both types are versatile and effective in pest control, offering targeted application and improved coverage compared to other formulations.</p></p>
