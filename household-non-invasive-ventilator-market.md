<p><p>The household non-invasive ventilator market includes three main types: CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure), BiPAP (Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure), and APAP (Automatic Positive Airway Pressure). CPAP provides a constant level of air pressure to keep the airways open during sleep. BiPAP offers two different levels of air pressure, making it easier to exhale. APAP automatically adjusts the air pressure based on the patient's breathing patterns. Each type is designed to treat different respiratory conditions and improve breathing efficiency at home.</p></p>
