<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>VOC (Volatile Organic Compound) meters are used to measure the presence of harmful gases in the atmosphere. Two common types of VOC meters in the market are PID (Photoionization Detector) and Metal-oxide Semiconductor. </p><p>PID meters detect VOCs by ionizing them with ultraviolet light, providing accurate and real-time measurements. On the other hand, Metal-oxide Semiconductor VOC meters detect gases based on changes in electrical conductivity. </p><p>Both technologies are widely used in industries such as manufacturing, healthcare, and environmental monitoring to ensure safety and compliance with regulations.</p></p>
