<p><p>CNC drilling machines can be categorized into automatic and semi-automatic types based on their level of automation. Automatic CNC drilling machines operate independently with minimal human intervention, allowing for efficient and high-volume production. On the other hand, semi-automatic CNC drilling machines require some level of manual input and supervision during the drilling process. While automatic machines are ideal for mass production and high precision drilling, semi-automatic machines are suitable for smaller scale operations that require more control over the drilling process.</p></p>
