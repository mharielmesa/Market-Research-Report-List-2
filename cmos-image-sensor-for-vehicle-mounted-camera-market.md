<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The CMOS Image Sensor for Vehicle-Mounted Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front-illuminated (FSI) CMOS Sensor</li><li>Backside-illuminated (BSI) CMOS Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Front-illuminated (FSI) CMOS sensors are a common type of image sensor used in vehicle-mounted cameras. These sensors have a traditional design where the photodiodes are located in the front of the sensor. On the other hand, backside-illuminated (BSI) CMOS sensors have a more advanced design where the photodiodes are located at the back of the sensor, allowing for better light sensitivity and image quality. BSI sensors are becoming more popular in the vehicle-mounted camera market due to their improved performance in low light conditions.</p></p>
