<p><strong>The Luxury Vinyl Flooring (LVT) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry-Back LVT</li><li>Loose-Lay LVT</li><li>SPC</li><li>WPC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Luxury Vinyl Flooring (LVT) is available in various market types, including Dry-Back LVT, Loose-Lay LVT, Stone Plastic Composite (SPC), and Wood Plastic Composite (WPC). Dry-Back LVT requires adhesive for installation, providing a permanent bond. Loose-Lay LVT is easy to install without adhesive, allowing for easy replacement. SPC is a rigid core LVT that is highly durable and water-resistant. WPC is a softer and more flexible LVT option with a wood-plastic composite core, offering cushioning and a warmer feel underfoot.</p></p>
