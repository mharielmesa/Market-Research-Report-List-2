<p><ul><li>Physical Extraction</li><li>Chemical Extraction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Physical extraction in the cold extractor market involves the use of mechanical methods such as pressing, grinding, or crushing to extract oils or juices from raw materials. This method is preferred for maintaining the natural flavors and nutritional value of the extract. On the other hand, chemical extraction utilizes solvents or other chemical substances to separate oils or compounds from the raw materials. While this method may be more efficient, there are concerns about potential chemical residues remaining in the final product.</p></p>
