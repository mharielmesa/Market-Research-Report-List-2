<p><strong>The Electrical Steel Coatings Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Grain-oriented</li><li>Non-grain oriented</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrical steel coatings are used in both grain-oriented and non-grain oriented applications in the market. Grain-oriented coatings are designed for use in transformers and other electrical applications where magnetic properties are critical and require uniform grain structure for efficient energy transmission. Non-grain oriented coatings, on the other hand, are used in motors, generators, and appliances where magnetic properties are important but grain structure is not as essential. Both types of coatings play a vital role in improving the performance and efficiency of electrical equipment.</p></p>
