<p><strong>The Gate Driver Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-Chip</li><li>Discrete Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gate driver chip market can be categorized into two types: On-Chip and Discrete Module market. In the On-Chip market, the gate driver is integrated onto the same chip as the power semiconductor device, offering a more compact and cost-effective solution. On the other hand, the Discrete Module market involves separate gate driver chips that are connected externally to the power semiconductor device. This allows for greater flexibility in system design and maintenance but may come at a higher cost.</p></p>
