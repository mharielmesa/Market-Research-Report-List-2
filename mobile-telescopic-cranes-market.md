<p><p>Mobile Telescopic Cranes are popular in two main market types, Wheeled Telescopic Cranes and Crawler Telescopic Cranes. Wheeled Telescopic Cranes are mounted on wheels, offering greater flexibility and mobility on various terrains. They are widely used in construction and industrial settings. On the other hand, Crawler Telescopic Cranes are mounted on tracks, providing stability and support on uneven surfaces. They are commonly used in heavy lifting and infrastructure projects. Both types of mobile telescopic cranes offer different advantages based on the specific needs of the job at hand.</p></p>
