<p><strong>The Veneer-Faced Panels for Flooring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Particleboard Faced</li><li>MDF Faced</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Veneer-faced panels for flooring come in two main types: particleboard-faced and MDF-faced. Particleboard-faced panels are made by bonding a thin layer of real wood veneer to a particleboard substrate, providing a cost-effective option with good durability. MDF-faced panels consist of medium-density fiberboard with a veneer layer on top, offering a smoother finish and more stability than particleboard. Both types are popular choices for flooring due to their affordability, versatility, and ease of installation.</p></p>
