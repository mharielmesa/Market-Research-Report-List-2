<p><strong>The Structural Glazing Spacer Tape Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Double Sided</li><li>Single Sided</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Structural Glazing Spacer Tape market includes two types: Double Sided and Single Sided. Double Sided Spacer Tape is designed with adhesive on both sides, providing strong bonding between glass panels and metal frames. It offers greater stability and durability in structural glazing applications. On the other hand, Single Sided Spacer Tape has adhesive only on one side, allowing for easier installation and repositioning. It is commonly used for temporary bonding or sealing purposes in construction projects. Both types cater to different requirements in the structural glazing industry.</p></p>
