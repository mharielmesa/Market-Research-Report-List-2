<p><p>Emergency Escape Breathing Devices are divided into two main types - Air Supplying Breathing Apparatus and Air Purifying Breathing Apparatus. Air Supplying Breathing Apparatus provide a clean air source through a compressed air cylinder or connection to an external air supply, ensuring a safe breathing environment in emergency situations. On the other hand, Air Purifying Breathing Apparatus filter out harmful contaminants from the surrounding air, providing a safe breathing experience for the user. Both types are crucial for ensuring safety in emergency escape scenarios.</p></p>
