<p><p>Biosurgery sealants are medical products used to control bleeding and promote tissue healing during surgical procedures. The two main types of biosurgery sealants are fibrin sealants and collagen sealants. Fibrin sealants work by mimicking the final stage of blood clotting, while collagen sealants promote wound healing by providing a scaffold for tissue growth. These sealants are commonly used in various surgical specialties such as orthopedic, cardiac, and general surgery to improve patient outcomes and reduce complications.</p></p>
