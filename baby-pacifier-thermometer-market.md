<p><p>Baby pacifier thermometers are available in two market types: Fahrenheit and Centigrade thermometers. Fahrenheit thermometers measure temperature on a scale where water freezes at 32 degrees and boils at 212 degrees, commonly used in the United States. Centigrade thermometers measure temperature on a scale where water freezes at 0 degrees and boils at 100 degrees, commonly used in most other countries. Both types of thermometers provide accurate temperature readings and are commonly used by parents to monitor their baby's health.</p></p>
