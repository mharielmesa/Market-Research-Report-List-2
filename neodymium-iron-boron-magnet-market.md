<p><strong>The Neodymium Iron Boron Magnet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bonded NdFeB</li><li>Sintered NdFeB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Neodymium Iron Boron (NdFeB) magnets are divided into two main market types: bonded NdFeB and sintered NdFeB. Bonded NdFeB magnets are made by mixing NdFeB powder with a polymer binder, allowing for complex shapes and sizes to be easily produced. Sintered NdFeB magnets, on the other hand, are made through a process of compressing and sintering NdFeB powder at high temperatures, resulting in magnets with higher magnetic properties. Both market types cater to different applications and industries based on their unique characteristics and production methods.</p></p>
