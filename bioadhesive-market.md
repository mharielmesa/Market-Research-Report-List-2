<p><p>Bioadhesives are divided into two main types: plant-based and animal-based. Plant-based bioadhesives are derived from natural sources such as starch, cellulose, and proteins, making them environmentally friendly and sustainable. On the other hand, animal-based bioadhesives are sourced from proteins like gelatin and collagen. Both types of bioadhesives offer strong adhesive properties and are used in various industries such as medical, packaging, and construction for bonding and sealing applications.</p></p>
