<p><p>Exhaust sensors are essential components in vehicles to monitor and control emissions. The market for exhaust sensors can be categorized into various types including Differential Pressure Sensors, Particulate Matter Sensors, NOx Sensors, O2 Sensors, and other sensors. Differential Pressure Sensors measure pressure differences across the diesel particulate filter. Particulate Matter Sensors detect and measure particles in exhaust gases. NOx Sensors monitor nitrogen oxide levels. O2 Sensors measure the oxygen content in exhaust gases. Other sensors may include temperature sensors, pressure sensors, and ammonia sensors to ensure efficient and clean emissions from vehicles.</p></p>
