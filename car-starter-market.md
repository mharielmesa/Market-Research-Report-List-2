<p><strong>The Car Starter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial (Sliding Armature)</li><li>Coaxial (Sliding Gear)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car starter market can be categorized into two main types: Axial (Sliding Armature) and Coaxial (Sliding Gear). Axial starters have a sliding armature design that moves back and forth along the axis of rotation, providing the necessary torque to start the engine. On the other hand, Coaxial starters have a sliding gear mechanism that engages with the engine flywheel to initiate the starting process. Both types of starters are commonly used in modern vehicles, offering reliable performance and efficient engine starting capabilities.</p></p>
