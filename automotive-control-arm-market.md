<p><ul><li>Multi-Link Suspension</li><li>Double Wishbone Suspension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive control arms are essential components in suspension systems, including multi-link suspension and double wishbone suspension. Multi-link suspension uses multiple control arms to connect the wheels to the chassis, allowing for independent movement and improved handling. Double wishbone suspension also utilizes control arms, with two angled arms connecting the wheel to the frame for better stability and control. Both of these suspension systems rely on control arms to provide adequate support and enhance the overall performance of the vehicle.</p></p>
