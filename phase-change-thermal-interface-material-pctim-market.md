<p><p>Phase Change Thermal Interface Material (PCTIM) comes in two main types: Thermal Pad and Thermal Paste. Thermal Pad is made of a soft silicone material that conforms to the surface of components, providing a reliable thermal connection. Thermal Paste, on the other hand, is a high-performance compound that fills in microscopic imperfections on surfaces to improve heat transfer. Both types are essential for ensuring efficient heat dissipation in electronic devices, such as computers and smartphones.</p></p>
