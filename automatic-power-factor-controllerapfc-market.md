<p><p>Automatic Power Factor Controllers (APFCs) are used to automatically maintain the power factor of electrical systems. There are two main types of APFCs: Active APFCs and Passive APFCs. Active APFCs use electronic switches such as transistors to adjust the power factor, while Passive APFCs use capacitors and inductors for the same purpose. Active APFCs are more efficient and accurate but also more expensive, while Passive APFCs are simpler and cheaper but less precise in power factor correction.</p></p>
