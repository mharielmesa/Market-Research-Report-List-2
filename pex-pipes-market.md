<p><ul><li>PE-Xa Pipe</li><li>PE-Xb Pipe</li><li>PE-Xc Pipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PEX pipes are a type of flexible plastic plumbing pipe used for residential and commercial plumbing systems. There are three main types of PEX pipes based on the manufacturing process: PE-Xa, PE-Xb, and PE-Xc. PE-Xa pipes are made through the peroxide method and are known for their superior strength and flexibility. PE-Xb pipes are manufactured using the silane method and offer good flexibility and resistance to high temperatures. PE-Xc pipes are produced using the electron beam method and are known for their excellent resistance to chlorine and other chemicals.</p></p>
