<p><p>Electrically Operated Diaphragm Pumps come in two main types: Single Acting and Double Acting. Single Acting pumps use a single diaphragm to move liquid through the pump, while Double Acting pumps use two diaphragm chambers to alternately draw in and expel liquid. Single Acting pumps are simpler in design and are often used for smaller applications, while Double Acting pumps offer more efficiency and higher flow rates, making them suitable for larger and more demanding tasks.</p></p>
