<p><p>Optoelectronic transistors are available in two main types: Tank Enclosed Type and Resin Enclosed Type. Tank Enclosed Type optoelectronic transistors are sealed in a metal tank for protection against external elements. On the other hand, Resin Enclosed Type optoelectronic transistors are encapsulated in resin for insulation and durability. Both types offer different levels of protection and are suitable for various applications in industries such as telecommunications, automotive, and healthcare.</p></p>
