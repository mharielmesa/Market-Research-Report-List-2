<p><p>Cloth self-adhesive tape is available in two main types - uncoated and coated. Uncoated cloth self-adhesive tape has a raw cloth surface, making it ideal for applications where high flexibility and breathability are important. On the other hand, coated cloth self-adhesive tape is treated with a layer of adhesive for improved durability and strength. Both types cater to different market demands, with uncoated tape being popular for medical and sports applications, while coated tape is commonly used in industrial and construction sectors.</p></p>
