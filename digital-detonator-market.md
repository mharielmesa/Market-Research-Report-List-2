<p><strong>The Digital Detonator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lnstantaneous Electric Detonator</li><li>Delay Electric Detonator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital detonator market includes two main types: instantaneous electric detonators and delay electric detonators. Instantaneous electric detonators are used for immediate detonation of explosives, providing precise timing for controlled blasting. Delay electric detonators, on the other hand, have adjustable time delays, allowing for sequential blasting and efficient rock fragmentation. Both types play a crucial role in mining, construction, and demolition industries, offering safer and more accurate blasting solutions compared to traditional methods.</p></p>
