<p><p>Organic Light-emitting Diode (OLED) Materials Market is categorized into two types - Passive Matrix OLED (PMOLED) and Active Matrix OLED (AMOLED) markets. PMOLED utilizes simpler driving electronics and is commonly used in small displays like mobile phones and MP3 players. On the other hand, AMOLED is more advanced and finds applications in larger displays such as TVs and monitors. Both types use organic compounds that emit light when an electric current is applied, offering high contrast ratios, wide viewing angles, and energy efficiency.</p></p>
