<p><p>Speaker cables are essential for transmitting audio signals from audio sources to speakers. There are two main types of speaker cables in the market: single mode and multimode cables. Single mode cables are designed for long-distance transmission with minimal signal loss, making them ideal for professional audio setups. On the other hand, multimode cables are more affordable and commonly used for shorter distances in home audio systems. Both types of cables are crucial for delivering high-quality sound and ensuring seamless audio transmission.</p></p>
