<p><p>Wireless Holter monitors come in various market types which include 1-channel, 2-channel, 3-channel, 7-channel, and 12-channel monitors. The number represents the amount of leads or electrodes that can be connected to the device to track the heart's electrical activity. 1-channel monitors are basic and suitable for simple monitoring, while 12-channel monitors offer more comprehensive data and are typically used in more complex medical situations. The choice of monitor depends on the specific monitoring needs and requirements of the patient.</p></p>
