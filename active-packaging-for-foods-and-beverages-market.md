<p><p>Active packaging for foods and beverages includes various types of technologies such as oxygen scavengers, moisture controllers, ethylene absorbers, edible films, antimicrobial agents, and others. Oxygen scavengers help in preserving the freshness of food by removing oxygen from the package, while moisture controllers maintain the desired humidity levels. Ethylene absorbers prolong the shelf life of fruits and vegetables, and edible films serve as a barrier to protect food. Antimicrobial agents prevent the growth of bacteria and other harmful microorganisms in food packaging.</p></p>
