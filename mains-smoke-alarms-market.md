<p><strong>The Mains Smoke Alarms Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Smoke Alarms</li><li>Ionization Smoke Alarms</li><li>Combination Smoke Alarms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photoelectric smoke alarms use a light beam to detect smoke particles, making them ideal for detecting smoldering fires. Ionization smoke alarms use a small amount of radioactive material to ionize the air, detecting fast-flaming fires. Combination smoke alarms combine both photoelectric and ionization technologies for comprehensive fire detection. These different types of smoke alarms cater to varying fire scenarios, ensuring optimal safety measures for different environments. The market for mains smoke alarms is diverse, offering consumers a range of options to suit their specific needs.</p></p>
