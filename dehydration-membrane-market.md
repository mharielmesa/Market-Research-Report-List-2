<p><p>Dehydration membrane market consists of two main types: gas dehydration and solvent dehydration. Gas dehydration involves removing water vapor from natural gas streams using specialized membranes, ensuring the gas meets quality standards for processing and transportation. On the other hand, solvent dehydration utilizes membranes to separate water from various solvents, such as alcohol or glycol, for industrial processes. Both markets are driven by the need for efficient dehydration solutions in energy, chemical, and other industries.</p></p>
