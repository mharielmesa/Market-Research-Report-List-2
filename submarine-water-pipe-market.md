<p><ul><li>Shallow Water Pipe</li><li>Deep Water Pipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Submarine water pipe market is divided into two main types based on the depth of water they operate in: Shallow water pipes and deep water pipes. Shallow water pipes are designed for use in coastal areas and relatively shallow waters, typically up to a depth of 200 meters. On the other hand, deep water pipes are built to withstand higher pressures and operate in deeper waters, typically beyond 200 meters. Each type caters to different environments and requirements, offering specialized solutions for specific depth conditions.</p></p>
