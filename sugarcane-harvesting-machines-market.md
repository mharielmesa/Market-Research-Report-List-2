<p><p>Sugarcane harvesting machines can be categorized into two main types: self-propelled machines and tractor-drawn machines. Self-propelled machines are equipped with their own engines and can operate independently in the field. They are often more expensive but offer higher efficiency and productivity. Tractor-drawn machines, on the other hand, need to be attached to a tractor for operation. While they may be more affordable, they are generally slower and less efficient compared to self-propelled machines. Both types of machines cater to different market needs and preferences.</p></p>
