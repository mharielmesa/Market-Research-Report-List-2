<p><p>Mineral Wool Acoustic Panels are classified into two main types: Glass Fiber Wool and Mineral Wool. Glass Fiber Wool panels are made from fine fibers of glass, offering excellent sound absorption properties. On the other hand, Mineral Wool panels are made from natural rock materials like basalt or diabase, providing good thermal insulation as well as sound absorption. Both types of panels are commonly used in construction and interior design applications for noise reduction and improving acoustic performance in buildings.</p></p>
