<p><p>Zero friction coatings are used to reduce friction between surfaces, increasing efficiency and durability. Two common types of coatings are PTFE-based coatings and MOS2 based coatings. PTFE-based coatings are known for their nonstick properties and high chemical resistance, making them ideal for applications such as cookware and industrial machinery. MOS2 based coatings, on the other hand, are known for their low friction coefficient and high load-carrying capacity, making them ideal for applications in automotive and aerospace industries.</p></p>
