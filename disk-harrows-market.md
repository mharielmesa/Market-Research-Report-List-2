<p><p>Disk harrows are agricultural implements used for breaking soil clods, leveling the field, and incorporating crop residue. There are three main types of disk harrows in the market: single action, offset type, and double action. Single action harrows have one set of disks that work independently, providing a basic level of soil preparation. Offset type harrows have two sets of disks arranged in an offset manner, allowing for better soil penetration and mixing. Double action harrows have two sets of disks that work in tandem, providing thorough soil cultivation and excellent residue incorporation.</p></p>
