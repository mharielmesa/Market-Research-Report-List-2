<p><p>Non-woven fabric market can be classified into different types based on the manufacturing process. Meltblown fabric is known for its fine filtration properties, Spunbonded fabric is durable and commonly used in disposable products. Spunlace fabric offers a soft feel and high absorbency, while Needle Punch fabric is robust and suitable for applications requiring strength. Other types include airlaid, wetlaid, and stitchbonded fabrics, each offering unique characteristics and suitable for diverse applications in industries such as healthcare, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
