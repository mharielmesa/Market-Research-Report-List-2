<p><p>See-through display technology is divided into two main types: LED and LCD. On the other hand, LCD see-through displays use liquid crystal technology to provide sharp image quality and energy efficiency. Both types of displays are commonly used in applications where transparency is required, such as retail showcases, museum exhibits, and smart windows. Each technology has its own advantages and is chosen based on specific project requirements.</p></p>
