<p><ul><li>Front Loaders</li><li>Rear Loaders</li><li>Side Loaders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Compression garbage trucks come in three main types: front loaders, rear loaders, and side loaders. Front loaders are designed to pick up containers from the front of the truck, making them ideal for commercial use. Rear loaders have a hydraulic press that compacts the waste in the back of the truck, making them efficient for residential areas. Side loaders are equipped with an automated arm that grabs and empties bins from the side of the truck, making them suitable for narrow streets or routes with low overhead clearance.</p></p>
