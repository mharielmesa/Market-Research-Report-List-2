<p><ul><li>POU RO Pure Water Machine</li><li>POE RO Pure Water Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>POU RO Pure Water Machine refers to Point-of-Use systems that are installed directly at the point where water is consumed, such as under the sink or on the countertop. On the other hand, POE RO Pure Water Machine stands for Point-of-Entry systems that are installed at the main water line to treat all water entering the house. Both types of water machines use reverse osmosis technology to purify water by removing contaminants, providing clean and safe drinking water for households.</p></p>
