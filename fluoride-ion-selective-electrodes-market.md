<p><p>Fluoride ion selective electrodes are devices used to measure the concentration of fluoride ions in a solution. There are two main types of electrodes used in this market: liquid membrane and PVC membrane. Liquid membrane electrodes use an organic solvent to extract fluoride ions, while PVC membrane electrodes have a polyvinyl chloride membrane that selectively binds fluoride ions. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, making them suitable for different applications in industries such as water treatment, medical diagnostics, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
