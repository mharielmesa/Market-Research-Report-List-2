<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Depth Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Structured-Light Camera</li><li>Stereo Vision Camera</li><li>Time-of-flight Camera</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Depth cameras are categorized into different types based on their working principle. Structured-light cameras use a pattern of light to capture depth information through triangulation. Stereo vision cameras utilize two lenses to create a 3D image by comparing the difference in perspective. Time-of-flight cameras measure the time taken for light to travel to an object and back to determine depth. Other types of depth cameras include infrared depth sensors and laser-based radar systems. Each type has its advantages and is used in various industries requiring accurate depth sensing technology.</p></p>
