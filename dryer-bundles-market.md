Tumble drying uses a rotating drum to dry materials quickly and evenly. Box drying utilizes heated air circulation within an enclosed space to dry materials efficiently. Drying tower involves passing materials through a tall tower with heated airflow to remove moisture. Each market type offers distinct features and benefits for various drying applications.</p></p>
