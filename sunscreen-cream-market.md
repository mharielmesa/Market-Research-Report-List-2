<p><p>Sunscreen cream market includes two main types: chemical sunscreens and physical sunscreens. Chemical sunscreens contain active ingredients that work by absorbing the sun's rays, while physical sunscreens contain minerals like zinc oxide or titanium dioxide that reflect and scatter the UV rays. Chemical sunscreens are popular for their lightweight feel and easy application, while physical sunscreens are preferred by those with sensitive skin or those looking for broad-spectrum protection. Both types offer protection against sun damage and skin cancer.</p></p>
