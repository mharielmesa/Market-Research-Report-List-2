<p><p>The Aviation Refueling market includes various types such as Mobile Tankers, Hydrant Dispensers, and Fueling Cabinets/Skids. Mobile Tankers are vehicles used to transport and refuel aircraft with fuel on the airfield. Hydrant Dispensers are fixed systems that deliver fuel from underground pipelines directly to aircraft at gates. Fueling Cabinets/Skids are compact fueling stations that can be easily transported and set up for refueling operations. Each type offers unique benefits and advantages to efficiently and effectively support aircraft refueling needs in the aviation industry.</p></p>
