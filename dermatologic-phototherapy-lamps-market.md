<p><p>Dermatologic phototherapy lamps can be categorized into two main types: local dermatologic phototherapy lamps and whole body dermatologic phototherapy lamps. Local dermatologic phototherapy lamps are designed to target specific areas of the skin for treatment, such as psoriasis plaques or eczema patches. On the other hand, whole body dermatologic phototherapy lamps are designed to treat larger areas of the body or the entire body at once. Both types of lamps emit controlled doses of ultraviolet light to help manage various skin conditions effectively.</p></p>
