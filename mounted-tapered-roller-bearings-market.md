<p><p>Mounted Tapered Roller Bearings are categorized into three types based on their design and structure. Single-row Mounted Tapered Roller Bearings are designed with a single row of rollers that run in parallel to each other. Double-row Mounted Tapered Roller Bearings have two rows of rollers that provide increased radial load capacity. Four-row Mounted Tapered Roller Bearings have four rows of rollers arranged in a specific configuration to support heavy radial and thrust loads. Each type is designed to meet different load requirements in various industrial applications.</p></p>
