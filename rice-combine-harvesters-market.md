<p><strong>The Rice Combine Harvesters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drum Threshing Rice Combine Harvesters</li><li>Axial-Flow Rice Combine Harvesters</li><li>Hybrid Rice Combine Harvesters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rice Combine Harvesters can be classified into three main types based on the method of threshing they use: Drum Threshing, Axial-Flow, and Hybrid. Drum Threshing harvesters have a rotating drum that separates the grain from the straw. Axial-Flow harvesters use a more advanced system that involves a rotor and concave to separate the grain. Hybrid harvesters combine features of both Drum Threshing and Axial-Flow technology to improve efficiency and output. Each type of harvester offers different benefits and capabilities depending on the specific needs of the farmer.</p></p>
