Mill finished aluminum extrusions have a smooth surface and are commonly used for industrial applications. Anodized aluminum extrusions have a protective oxide layer that increases durability and corrosion resistance, making them suitable for outdoor use. Powder-coated aluminum extrusions are coated with a dry powder that is cured to create a durable finish with a wide range of colors and textures, making them ideal for architectural and decorative applications.</p></p>
