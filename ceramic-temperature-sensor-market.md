<p><ul><li>Thermistor Temperature Sensor</li><li>Thermocouple Temperature Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic temperature sensors are classified into two main types - Thermistor temperature sensors and Thermocouple temperature sensors. Thermistor sensors utilize the change in electrical resistance of ceramic material with temperature to measure temperature accurately. Thermocouple sensors work on the principle of generating a voltage difference between two different metals when exposed to heat. Both types of ceramic temperature sensors are widely used in various industries for monitoring and controlling temperature in applications such as HVAC systems, automotive engines, and industrial processes.</p></p>
