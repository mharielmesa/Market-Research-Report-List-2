<p><strong>The HV Bushing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Condenser Type</li><li>Non-Condenser Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HV bushing market is divided into two main types - condenser type and non-condenser type. Condenser type bushings are mainly used in oil-filled transformers and have a condenser core to control voltage distribution. On the other hand, non-condenser type bushings are used in dry-type transformers and do not have a condenser core. Both types of bushings play a crucial role in maintaining proper insulation and voltage distribution in high voltage systems.</p></p>
