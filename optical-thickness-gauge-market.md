<p><p>Optical thickness gauge market is divided into two types based on the light source used - halogen light source and Infrared LD. Halogen light source optical thickness gauges use a high-intensity halogen lamp to provide a consistent light source for accurate measurements. On the other hand, Infrared LD optical thickness gauges utilize an infrared laser diode to emit a focused beam of light for precise measurements. Both types of gauges are used in various industries for measuring the thickness of materials with high precision.</p></p>
