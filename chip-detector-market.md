<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Multi-channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chip Detector Market Types include Single Channel and Multi-channel detectors. Single Channel detectors have one sensor that detects particles in the oil system, providing a simplified monitoring system. On the other hand, Multi-channel detectors have multiple sensors that can detect particles of different sizes, providing more comprehensive monitoring capabilities. Single Channel detectors are typically used in simpler systems where only basic monitoring is required, while Multi-channel detectors are preferred for more complex systems that require detailed particle analysis.</p></p>
