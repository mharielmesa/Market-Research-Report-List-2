<p><p>Titanium Silicon targets are commonly used in sputtering processes for thin film deposition in various industries. Plane targets are stationary targets that are commonly used for large area deposition, such as in the production of flat panel displays. Rotating targets, on the other hand, are targets that are spun during the sputtering process, allowing for more uniform film deposition and higher utilization of the target material. They are often used in applications where high precision and control over the deposition process are required, such as in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
