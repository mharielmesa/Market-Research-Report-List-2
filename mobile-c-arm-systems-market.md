<p><p>Mobile C-arm systems are classified into different types based on their sizes and applications. Little C-arms are compact and ideal for orthopedic surgeries, dental procedures, and pain management interventions. They offer high image quality and maneuverability in tight spaces. Peripheral interventional C-arm systems are larger and used for advanced procedures such as vascular, neuro, and cardiac interventions. They provide exceptional image quality and real-time imaging capabilities for precise guidance during complex procedures. Both types of mobile C-arm systems play a crucial role in modern medical settings.</p></p>
