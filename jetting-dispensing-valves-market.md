<p><p>Jetting dispensing valves are used for precision fluid dispensing in various industries. There are two main types based on the viscosity of the material being dispensed. Low to medium viscosity jetting dispensing valves are designed for fluids with thinner consistency, such as adhesives and sealants. On the other hand, medium to high viscosity jetting dispensing valves are suitable for thicker materials like solder paste and encapsulants. Both types ensure accurate and efficient dispensing of fluids for different applications.</p></p>
