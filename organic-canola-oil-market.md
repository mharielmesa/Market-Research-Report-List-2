<p><ul><li>Pressed</li><li>Leaching</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Organic Canola Oil is typically divided into two main market types: pressed and leaching. Pressed Canola Oil is extracted from the canola seeds using mechanical pressing techniques, which preserves the natural nutrients and flavors of the oil. On the other hand, leaching Canola Oil involves a chemical solvent extraction process, which may result in a higher oil yield but can also strip away some of the oil's natural qualities. Consumers may choose between these two market types based on their preferences for extraction methods and resulting oil qualities.</p></p>
