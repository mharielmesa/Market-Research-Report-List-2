<p><p>Non-stick coating chemicals are primarily categorized into four types - PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene), PFA (perfluoroalkoxy), FEP (fluorinated ethylene propylene), and other coatings. PTFE is known for its excellent non-stick properties and high heat resistance, making it suitable for cookware. PFA offers improved durability and resistance to chemical exposure. FEP coatings are known for their low friction properties. Other coatings include ceramic, silicone, and enamel, each offering unique advantages such as scratch resistance and easy cleaning.</p></p>
