<p><p>Surf fishing rod market can be divided into two main types: spinning rod and casting rod. Spinning rods are designed with the reel hanging underneath the rod and are easier to use for beginners. They are highly versatile and can be used for a variety of fishing techniques. Casting rods, on the other hand, have the reel on top of the rod and are commonly used by experienced anglers for more precise casting. Both types cater to different fishing preferences and styles, offering options for anglers of all skill levels.</p></p>
