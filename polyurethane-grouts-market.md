<p><p>Polyurethane grouts are widely used in construction and infrastructure repair projects. There are two main types of polyurethane grouts: waterborne and solventborne. Waterborne polyurethane grouts are environmentally friendly and have low VOC emissions, making them suitable for indoor use. Solventborne polyurethane grouts, on the other hand, offer superior adhesion and quicker curing times, making them ideal for outdoor applications and projects requiring faster turnaround times. Both types of polyurethane grouts provide excellent strength and durability for various construction applications.</p></p>
