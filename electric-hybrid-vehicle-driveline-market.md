<p><p>Electric & Hybrid Vehicle Driveline Market includes various types such as Series Driveline, Parallel Driveline, Power Split Driveline, Electric Driveline and Others. Series Driveline is where the engine only charges the battery, while the electric motor propels the vehicle. Parallel Driveline involves both the engine and the electric motor providing power to the wheels simultaneously. Power Split Driveline combines series and parallel drivelines for optimal efficiency. Electric Driveline exclusively uses electric power for propulsion. Other types include innovative driveline technologies aimed at improving performance and efficiency.</p></p>
