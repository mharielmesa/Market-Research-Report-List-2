<p><p>Harvesting headers are essential equipment for gathering crops in agricultural settings. Two common types of harvesting headers are rigid and flex headers. Rigid headers have a fixed cutting platform, providing stability and precision for crops that grow upright. Flex headers, on the other hand, have flexible cutting platforms that can adapt to uneven terrain and varying crop heights, allowing for more efficient and effective harvesting. Both types cater to different market needs and preferences within the agricultural sector.</p></p>
