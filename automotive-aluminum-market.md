<p><p>Automotive aluminum market consists of various types including cast aluminum, rolled aluminum, and extruded aluminum. Cast aluminum is shaped by pouring molten aluminum into a mold, while rolled aluminum is produced by passing aluminum sheet through rollers. Extruded aluminum is created by pushing aluminum through a die to create a specific shape. Cast aluminum is commonly used in engine parts, rolled aluminum in body panels, and extruded aluminum in structural components of automobiles. Each type serves a specific purpose in the automotive industry.</p></p>
