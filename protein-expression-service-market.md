<p><p>Protein expression services can be categorized into different types based on the expression systems used. The most common types include Prokaryotic Expression Systems, which use bacterial cells like E. coli for protein production, Mammalian Cell Expression Systems, utilizing mammalian cells for higher protein folding and post-translational modifications, Yeast Cell Expression Systems, known for their ease of genetic manipulation, and Other systems like insect cells or plant cells. Each system has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the protein being expressed.</p></p>
