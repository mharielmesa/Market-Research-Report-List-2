<p><p>Fire Protection Coatings Market Types include Cementitious Coatings and Intumescent Coatings. Cementitious Coatings are used to protect structural steel and concrete from fire damage by providing a durable barrier that resists high temperatures. Intumescent Coatings, on the other hand, expand when exposed to heat, forming a protective char layer that insulates the substrate and slows down the spread of fire. Both types of coatings are essential for safeguarding buildings and structures from fire hazards.</p></p>
