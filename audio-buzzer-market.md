<p><p>The audio buzzer market is primarily divided into three main types: piezo buzzer, electromagnetic buzzer, and others. Piezo buzzers use a piezoelectric crystal to produce sound waves when it is electrically charged. Electromagnetic buzzers, on the other hand, use an electromagnetic coil and a diaphragm to produce sound. The "others" category includes various types of buzzers such as magnetic buzzers and mechanical buzzers. Each type of buzzer has its own unique characteristics and applications within the audio industry.</p></p>
