<p><strong>The Adas Driving Control Unit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Perception Control Unit</li><li>Decision Control Unit</li><li>Actuator Control Unit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Adas Driving Control Unit market consists of three main types: Perception Control Unit, Decision Control Unit, and Actuator Control Unit. The Perception Control Unit is responsible for collecting and interpreting data from various sensors to understand the vehicle's surroundings. The Decision Control Unit processes this information to make decisions and determine the appropriate response. The Actuator Control Unit is then responsible for executing these decisions by controlling the vehicle's actuators such as brakes and steering. Together, these units work to enhance the safety and efficiency of autonomous driving systems.</p></p>
