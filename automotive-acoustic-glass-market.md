<p><p>Automotive acoustic glass market includes various types such as double deck glass and three layers of glass. Double deck glass consists of two layers of glass separated by a layer of air, providing enhanced sound insulation properties. On the other hand, three layers of glass feature an additional layer for even more sound reduction capabilities. These types of acoustic glass are designed to reduce noise and improve overall driving experience by minimizing external noise penetration into the vehicle cabin.</p></p>
