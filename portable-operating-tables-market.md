<p><p>Portable operating tables are available in different types based on their operating mechanism - Manual, Electric, Hydraulic, and Electro-hydraulic. Manual tables are operated manually by the user, while electric tables are powered by electricity for easy adjustment. Hydraulic tables use hydraulic fluid to adjust height and position, providing smooth movements. Electro-hydraulic tables combine the benefits of both electric and hydraulic mechanisms for precise and efficient adjustments. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the medical field.</p></p>
