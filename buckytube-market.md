<p><ul><li>Single-walled</li><li>Multi-walled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Buckytubes are carbon nanotubes that come in two main market types: single-walled and multi-walled. Single-walled buckytubes consist of just one layer of carbon atoms arranged in a cylindrical structure, offering unique properties such as high strength and electrical conductivity. On the other hand, multi-walled buckytubes have multiple layers of carbon atoms arranged concentrically, providing enhanced mechanical and thermal properties. Both types of buckytubes have various applications in industries such as electronics, aerospace, and medical devices.</p></p>
