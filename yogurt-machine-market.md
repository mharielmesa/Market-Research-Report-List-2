<p><p>Yogurt machines come in two main types: full-automatic and semi-automatic. Full-automatic machines are designed to perform all yogurt-making processes automatically, from heating and fermenting to cooling and storing. They are convenient and require minimal intervention from the user. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some manual steps from the user, such as adjusting the temperature and adding cultures. They offer more control over the yogurt-making process but require more effort and attention. Each type caters to different preferences and needs in the market.</p></p>
