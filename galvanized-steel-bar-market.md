<p><p>Galvanized Steel Bar Market offers two main types: Conventional Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar and Continuous Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar. Conventional Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar involves the immersing of rebar in a bath of molten zinc to create a protective coating. In contrast, Continuous Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar uses a continuous galvanizing process to coat the rebar. The market for these products caters to industries requiring corrosion-resistant and durable materials, such as construction and infrastructure development.</p></p>
