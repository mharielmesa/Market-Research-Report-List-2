<p><p>Sugar beet harvesters are available in three main types in the market. Self-propelled sugar beet harvesters are standalone machines with their own engine and can operate independently in the field. Trailed sugar beet harvesters are towed by a tractor and are more suitable for smaller farms. Semi-mounted sugar beet harvesters are partially attached to a tractor and are versatile for various field sizes. Each type of harvester has its own advantages and is used based on the specific needs of the farmer or farm operation.</p></p>
