<p><p>Polydimethylsiloxane (PDMS) is a versatile silicone-based polymer widely used in various industries. In the market, PDMS is available in two main types based on viscosity: High Viscosity and Low Viscosity. High viscosity PDMS is thicker and more suitable for applications requiring a more solid-like consistency, such as in sealants and adhesives. On the other hand, low viscosity PDMS is more fluid and is commonly used in applications such as lubricants and coatings where a thinner, more spreadable consistency is desired.</p></p>
