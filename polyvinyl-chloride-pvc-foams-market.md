<p><p>Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) Foams are classified into two main types: PVC Crosslinked Foams and PVC Non-crosslinked Foams. PVC Crosslinked Foams are foam materials that have chemical bonds formed between polymer chains, resulting in a strong and durable structure. On the other hand, PVC Non-crosslinked Foams do not have chemical bonds between polymer chains, leading to a softer and more flexible material. Both types of PVC foams have unique properties and applications in various industries such as construction, automotive, and packaging.</p></p>
