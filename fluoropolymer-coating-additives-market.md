<p><p>Fluoropolymer Coating Additives market includes Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE), Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene (FEP), and Polyvinylidene Fluoride (PVDF) types. PTFE is known for its excellent chemical resistance and low friction properties, making it ideal for applications requiring non-stick surfaces. FEP offers a combination of high clarity, flexibility, and chemical resistance, making it suitable for wire insulation and coatings. PVDF provides high thermal stability, weather resistance, and strong mechanical properties, making it a preferred choice for architectural coatings and chemical processing equipment.</p></p>
