<p><p>Endovascular therapy devices are minimally invasive tools used to treat various cardiovascular diseases. These devices are designed to be inserted into blood vessels via a catheter, allowing for targeted treatment without the need for traditional open surgeries. They are commonly used in procedures such as angioplasty, stenting, and embolization to restore blood flow, reinforce vessels, or prevent bleeding.</p><p>The market for endovascular therapy devices is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years. The increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, along with advancements in technology and the growing preference for minimally invasive procedures, are key factors driving this growth. In addition, a rising geriatric population and improving healthcare infrastructure in developing countries are also contributing to the expansion of the market.</p><p>The Endovascular Therapy Device Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.80% during the forecast period. Some of the latest trends in the market include the development of drug-eluting stents, bioresorbable stents, and robotic-assisted endovascular procedures to improve patient outcomes and reduce complications.
