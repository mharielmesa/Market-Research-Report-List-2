<p><p>Sulfur textile fiber dyes are categorized into three main types: Conventional Sulphur Dyes, Solubilized Sulphur Dyes, and Leuco Sulphur Dyes. Conventional Sulphur Dyes are water-insoluble and require reducing agents for fixation, while Solubilized Sulphur Dyes are pre-reduced and water-soluble. Leuco Sulphur Dyes are a newer type that offer improved color fastness properties. Each type of sulfur dye has its own unique qualities and benefits, catering to different requirements in the textile industry and providing a wide range of color options for textile fibers.</p></p>
