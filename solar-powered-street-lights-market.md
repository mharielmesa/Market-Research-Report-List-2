<p><strong>The Solar Powered Street Lights Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standalone</li><li>Grid Connected</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solar powered street lights can be categorized into two main types - standalone and grid connected. Standalone street lights operate independently from the electricity grid, using solar panels and batteries to store and provide power. They are typically used in remote areas where grid connectivity is limited. Grid connected street lights, on the other hand, are connected to the main electricity grid and rely on both solar power and grid electricity to function. They are commonly used in urban areas where grid connectivity is easily accessible.</p></p>
