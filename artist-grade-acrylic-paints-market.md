<p><strong>The Artist Grade Acrylic Paints Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluid Acrylics</li><li>Heavy-Body Acrylics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Artist grade acrylic paints can be categorized into two main types - Fluid Acrylics and Heavy-Body Acrylics. Fluid acrylics are known for their smooth consistency and ability to flow easily, making them ideal for techniques like pouring and glazing. On the other hand, heavy-body acrylics have a thick, buttery consistency that holds brush strokes well and is often used for impasto techniques. Both types cater to different preferences and techniques within the artist community, offering a wide range of options for creating stunning works of art.</p></p>
