<p><p>Fuel cell platinum-based catalyst market can be categorized into two main types: Pt alloy catalyst and Pt/C catalyst. Pt alloy catalysts consist of platinum combined with other metals to enhance catalytic activity and durability. On the other hand, Pt/C catalysts combine platinum with carbon support materials to improve performance and reduce cost. Both types of catalysts are widely used in fuel cells to facilitate the electrochemical reactions that produce electricity, making them crucial components in the clean energy industry.</p></p>
