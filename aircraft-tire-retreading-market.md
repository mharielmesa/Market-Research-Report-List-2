<p><strong>The Aircraft Tire Retreading Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre-Cure</li><li>Mold-Cure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aircraft tire retreading market consists of two main types: Pre-Cure and Mold-Cure. Pre-Cure retreading involves applying a new tread on a tire casing using a pre-cured tread rubber strip and is the most commonly used method due to its cost-effectiveness. Mold-Cure retreading, on the other hand, involves placing the tire in a mold with raw tread rubber, which is then cured to form the new tread. Mold-Cure retreading is known for providing better bonding and longer-lasting treads compared to Pre-Cure retreading.</p></p>
