Low Viscosity HPMC is commonly used as a thickener and stabilizer in various food products. Middle Viscosity HPMC is often used in dairy products and baked goods for its ability to improve texture and moisture retention. High Viscosity HPMC is ideal for applications requiring a strong thickening and gelling agent, such as sauces and dressings. Each type of HPMC offers unique properties and benefits for different food applications.</p></p>
