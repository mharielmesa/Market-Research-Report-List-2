<p><p>Quantitative funds utilize various market types to generate profits. Trend following funds capitalize on market momentum, while countertrend strategies aim to profit from reversals. Statistical arbitrage funds exploit pricing discrepancies using mathematical models.</p><p>Convertible arbitrage involves taking advantage of differences in pricing between a convertible bond and its underlying stock. Fixed income arbitrage seeks to profit from interest rate differentials. Commodity spread trades involve simultaneous purchase and sale of related commodities in different markets.</p><p>Other markets may include volatility trading, event-driven strategies, and global macro approaches.</p></p>
