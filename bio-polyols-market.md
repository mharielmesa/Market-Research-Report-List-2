<p><p>Bio Polyols are a type of polyol derived from renewable resources such as vegetable oils or biomass. There are two main types of Bio Polyols in the market - Polyether Polyols and Polyester Polyols. Polyether Polyols are created through the addition of ethylene oxide or propylene oxide to a compound with active hydrogen atoms, while Polyester Polyols are produced through the esterification of organic acids and glycols. Both types of Bio Polyols are widely used in the production of polyurethane foams, coatings, adhesives, and sealants.</p></p>
