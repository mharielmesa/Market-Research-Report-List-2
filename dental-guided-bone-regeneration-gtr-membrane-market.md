<p><p>Dental Guided Bone Regeneration membranes are used to promote bone regeneration and tissue healing in dental procedures. There are multiple types of membranes available, including absorbable membranes, non-absorbable membranes, collagen membranes, metal membranes, synthetic membranes, and periosteal allografts. Absorbable membranes are gradually absorbed by the body, while non-absorbable membranes remain in place long-term. Collagen membranes are derived from natural sources, while synthetic membranes are man-made. Metal membranes are durable and can provide structural support. Periosteal allografts are derived from donated human tissue.</p></p>
