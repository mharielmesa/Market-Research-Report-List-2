<p><ul><li>Gelling Agents</li><li>Friction Reducers</li><li>Surfactants</li><li>Corrosion & Scale Inhibitors</li><li>Acids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oilfield stimulation chemicals market includes various types such as gelling agents, friction reducers, surfactants, corrosion & scale inhibitors, and acids. Gelling agents help in thickening the fluid to improve hydraulic fracturing efficiency. Friction reducers reduce friction between fluid and wellbore, enhancing flow efficiency. Surfactants help in reducing surface tension, aiding in better fluid flow. Corrosion & scale inhibitors prevent the formation of scale and protect equipment from corrosion. Acids are used to dissolve minerals and remove blockages in the reservoir, improving production.</p></p>

<p><p>Oilfield stimulation chemicals are utilized in various applications such as hydraulic fracturing, matrix acidization, and acid fracking to enhance the productivity of oil and gas wells. Hydraulic fracturing involves injecting fluids at high pressure to create fractures in the rock formation, allowing for better oil and gas flow. Matrix acidization is used to dissolve minerals and restore permeability in the formation. Acid fracking involves using acid-based fluids to break down rock and improve well productivity. These applications play a crucial role in maximizing oil and gas production.</p></p>
