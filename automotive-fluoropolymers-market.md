<p><p>Automotive fluoropolymers are classified into several types based on their chemical composition and properties. Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) is known for its high resistance to heat and chemicals. Ethylene Tetrafluoroethylene (ETFE) is a durable and weather-resistant material. Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene (FEP) offers excellent electrical properties. Fluoroelastomer (FKM) is a type of rubber with high resistance to chemicals and extreme temperatures. Other types of fluoropolymers in the market cater to specific applications in the automotive industry, providing a wide range of options for manufacturers.</p></p>
