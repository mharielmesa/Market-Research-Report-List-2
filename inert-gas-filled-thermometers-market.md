<p><ul><li>Rigid Stem Type</li><li>Capillary Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inert gas filled thermometers are categorized into two main types: rigid stem type and capillary type. Rigid stem type thermometers have a fixed stem length and are suitable for measuring temperature in a specific point. On the other hand, capillary type thermometers have a flexible capillary tube that allows for measurement of temperature at a distance from the thermometer. Both types rely on inert gas to measure temperature accurately and are commonly used in industrial applications where precise temperature monitoring is essential.</p></p>
