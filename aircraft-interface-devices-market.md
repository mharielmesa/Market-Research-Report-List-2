<p><ul><li>Wired Aircraft Interface Devices</li><li>Wireless Aircraft Interface Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aircraft Interface Devices (AID) market can be categorized into two main types: Wired Aircraft Interface Devices and Wireless Aircraft Interface Devices. Wired AID utilize physical cables for data transfer and communication within an aircraft, while Wireless AID use wireless technology such as Bluetooth or Wi-Fi for connectivity. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, with Wired AID typically offering more secure and reliable connections, while Wireless AID provide greater flexibility and convenience in terms of installation and maintenance.</p></p>
