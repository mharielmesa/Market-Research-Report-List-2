<p><ul><li>Single-Sided Boards</li><li>Double-Sided Boards</li><li>Multilayer Circuit Board</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Printed Circuit Boards (PCB) come in various types based on the number of layers they have. Single-sided boards have components and connections on only one side, while double-sided boards have components and connections on both sides. Multilayer circuit boards have multiple layers of conductive material sandwiched between insulating layers, allowing for more complex circuits and higher component density. Each type of PCB market caters to different applications and requirements, from simple electronics to advanced technology.</p></p>
