<p><ul><li>Titanium oxide type</li><li>Zirconia type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive oxygen sensors are categorized into two main types: titanium oxide type and zirconia type. The titanium oxide type sensors use a titanium oxide semiconducting material to measure oxygen levels in exhaust gases, while zirconia type sensors utilize a zirconia ceramic element. The titanium oxide type sensors are known for their fast response time and high durability, making them popular in racing and high-performance vehicles. On the other hand, zirconia type sensors are more commonly used in everyday vehicles due to their accuracy and ability to operate in varying temperature conditions.</p></p>
