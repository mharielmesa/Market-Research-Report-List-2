<p><strong>The Train Door Lights Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ceiling Lamp</li><li>Wall Lamp</li><li>Caudal Lamp</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The train door lights market can be categorized into three types based on their placements within the train: Ceiling Lamp, Wall Lamp, and Caudal Lamp. Ceiling lamps are installed on the ceiling of the train, providing overhead illumination. Wall lamps are mounted on the walls of the train, offering light to passengers entering or exiting the train. Caudal lamps are placed near the train doors, typically at the back end of the train, to ensure visibility and safety during boarding and alighting.</p></p>
