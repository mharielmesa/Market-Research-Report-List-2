<p><strong>The Dental CBCT Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Small Field</li><li>Medium Field</li><li>Large Field</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental CBCT systems are categorized into three types based on the field of view they offer: Small Field, Medium Field, and Large Field. Small Field systems provide detailed images of specific areas like single tooth or root canal, while Medium Field systems offer a broader view of multiple teeth and surrounding structures. Large Field systems provide the widest coverage and are typically used for diagnosing more complex dental issues involving the entire jaw or craniofacial region. Each type caters to different clinical needs and treatment planning requirements in dentistry.</p></p>
