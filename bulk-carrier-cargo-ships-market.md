<p><ul><li>Geared bulk carriers</li><li>Combined carriers</li><li>Gearless carriers</li><li>Self-dischargers</li><li>Lakers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bulk carrier cargo ships serve different market types based on their capabilities and features. Geared bulk carriers are equipped with cranes for self-loading and unloading of cargo. Combined carriers have both bulk and container carrying capacities. Gearless carriers rely on shoreside equipment for loading and unloading. Self-dischargers have onboard conveyor systems for efficient cargo handling. Lakers operate in the Great Lakes region and are specially designed for navigating the freshwater bodies. Each market type caters to specific cargo transportation needs and requirements.</p></p>
