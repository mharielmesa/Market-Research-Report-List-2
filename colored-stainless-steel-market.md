<p><strong>The Colored Stainless Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spectral Stainless Steel</li><li>PVD Coated Stainless Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Colored stainless steel can be categorized into two main types: Spectral Stainless Steel and PVD Coated Stainless Steel. Spectral stainless steel achieves its color through a process that alters the surface of the metal, resulting in a vibrant and reflective appearance. On the other hand, PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) coated stainless steel involves a vacuum coating process to apply a layer of colored material onto the steel surface, offering a durable and scratch-resistant finish. These technologies cater to the growing demand for aesthetically pleasing and customizable stainless steel products.</p></p>
