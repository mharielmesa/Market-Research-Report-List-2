<p><p>Industrial turbine oils are used to lubricate and cool turbines in various industrial applications. There are two main types of turbine oils in the market: synthetic oil and mineral oil. Synthetic oils are made from chemical compounds and offer superior performance and longer lifespan compared to mineral oils, which are derived from natural crude oil. Synthetic oils have high resistance to oxidation, thermal stability, and better fluidity at low temperatures. Mineral oils, on the other hand, are more affordable and widely used in many industrial applications.</p></p>
