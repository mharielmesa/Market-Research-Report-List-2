<p><ul><li>Ethylene Acrylates</li><li>Polyacrylates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acrylic Based Elastomers can be categorized into two main types - Ethylene Acrylates and Polyacrylates. Ethylene Acrylates are copolymers of ethylene and acrylic acid, offering good flexibility, impact resistance, and weatherability. On the other hand, Polyacrylates are polymers derived from acrylic acid, providing excellent durability, chemical resistance, and high-temperature performance. Both types of acrylic-based elastomers are widely used in various industries such as automotive, construction, and electronics for their unique properties and versatility.</p></p>
