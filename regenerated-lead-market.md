<p><ul><li>Pyrometallurgy</li><li>Solid Phase Electrolytic Reduction</li><li>Wet Smelting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Regenerated lead can be produced through three main market types: pyrometallurgy, solid phase electrolytic reduction, and wet smelting. Pyrometallurgy involves heating lead-containing materials to high temperatures to extract pure lead. Solid phase electrolytic reduction uses an electrolytic cell to produce lead from solid lead oxide. Wet smelting involves using chemical reactions in a liquid medium to separate lead from other materials. Each of these methods has its own set of benefits and drawbacks, making them suitable for different applications in the lead industry.</p></p>
