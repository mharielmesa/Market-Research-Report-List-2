<p><p>Flexible Composite Pipes (FCP) are innovative piping systems that offer enhanced flexibility and durability compared to traditional rigid pipes. Non-metallic FCPs are made from composite materials such as reinforced thermoplastic or thermosetting resins, providing resistance to corrosion and chemicals. On the other hand, Metallic FCPs incorporate metal layers for added strength and are suitable for high-pressure applications. Both types of FCPs are gaining popularity in various industries including oil and gas, offshore, and chemical processing due to their advantages in installation, maintenance, and long-term performance.</p></p>
