<p><p>Digital gloss meters are devices used to measure the gloss level of a surface. There are three main types of digital gloss meters available in the market: single-angle, dual angles, and multi-angles. Single-angle gloss meters measure the gloss level at one specific angle, providing a basic measurement. Dual angles gloss meters measure gloss at two angles, offering more accurate readings for different surfaces. Multi-angles gloss meters measure gloss at multiple angles, providing comprehensive data for a wide range of surfaces and materials.</p></p>
