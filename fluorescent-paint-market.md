<p><p>Fluorescent paint is divided into two main types: organic fluorescent paint and inorganic fluorescent paint. Organic fluorescent paint is known for its bright and vibrant colors, while inorganic fluorescent paint is valued for its longer-lasting properties and resistance to fading. Both types of fluorescent paint are popular for use in various applications such as signage, art, safety markings, and decorations.</p></p>
