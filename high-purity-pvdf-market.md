<p><p>High purity PVDF market consists of two main types: PVDF granules and PVDF powder. PVDF granules are small pellets of high purity PVDF resin used in various applications such as coatings, membranes, and solar panels. On the other hand, PVDF powder is a fine powder form of PVDF resin that is commonly used in the production of high-performance coatings, films, and pipes. Both forms of PVDF offer high purity and excellent chemical resistance, making them popular choices in industries such as electronics, construction, and automotive.</p></p>
