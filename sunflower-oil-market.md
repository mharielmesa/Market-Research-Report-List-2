<p><p>Sunflower oil is categorized into three main market types based on their oleic acid content. Linoleic oil has a high level of polyunsaturated fatty acids, making it a common choice for cooking and baking. Mid-oleic oil has a balance of polyunsaturated and monounsaturated fatty acids, offering a healthier option. High-oleic oil contains mostly monounsaturated fats, making it more stable for frying at high temperatures. Each market type caters to different consumer preferences and dietary needs in the culinary world.</p></p>
