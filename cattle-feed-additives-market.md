<p><p>The cattle feed additives market can be categorized into various types such as acidifiers, antibiotics, antioxidants, and others. Acidifiers are substances added to feed to maintain the pH balance in the digestive system of cattle. Antibiotics are used to prevent diseases and promote growth in cattle. Antioxidants help in preventing the oxidation of feed ingredients. Other additives include enzymes, probiotics, and vitamins that enhance the overall health and performance of cattle. These additives play a crucial role in improving the efficiency and productivity of cattle farming operations.</p></p>
