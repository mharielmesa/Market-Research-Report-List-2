<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Clutch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Friction Clutch</li><li>Dry Friction Clutch</li><li>Electromagnetic Clutch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive clutch market consists of three main types: wet friction clutch, dry friction clutch, and electromagnetic clutch. Wet friction clutches use oil to reduce friction and dissipate heat, making them ideal for high-performance vehicles. Dry friction clutches do not use oil and are commonly found in passenger cars and commercial vehicles. Electromagnetic clutches use magnetic force to engage and disengage the clutch, making them efficient and durable for a wide range of automotive applications. Each type offers unique benefits and caters to different vehicle requirements.</p></p>
