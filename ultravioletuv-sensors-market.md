<p><p>The Ultraviolet (UV) Sensors market is segmented into three main types: UVA sensors, UVB sensors, and others. UVA sensors primarily detect long-wave ultraviolet light, which is responsible for skin aging and can penetrate deeper into the skin. UVB sensors, on the other hand, detect medium-wave ultraviolet light, which causes sunburn and skin damage. The "others" category includes sensors that can detect a wider range of ultraviolet light wavelengths beyond UVA and UVB, offering more comprehensive protection against harmful UV rays.</p></p>
