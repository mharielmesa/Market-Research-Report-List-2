<p><p>Commercial dough machines come in two main types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic machines are fully automated and require minimal human intervention to operate. These machines are ideal for high-volume production and consistently produce uniform dough products. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some manual input for operation, such as feeding the dough into the machine or adjusting settings. These machines are more suitable for smaller scale operations or where more customization and control over the dough production process is desired.</p></p>
