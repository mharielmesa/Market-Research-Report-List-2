<p><p>The Automotive MEMS Inertial Sensor Market includes MEMS Accelerometers, MEMS Gyroscopes, and MEMS IMUs. MEMS Accelerometers measure acceleration forces, MEMS Gyroscopes detect angular rotation, and MEMS IMUs combine both accelerometer and gyroscope functionalities to provide comprehensive motion sensing capabilities in vehicles. These sensors play a crucial role in enhancing safety, stability, and overall performance of automotive systems by providing real-time data on vehicle movement, orientation, and vibration. The growing demand for advanced driver assistance systems and autonomous vehicles is driving the market growth of these sensor types.</p></p>
