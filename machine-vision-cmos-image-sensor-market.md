<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Machine Vision CMOS Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolling Shutter</li><li>Global Shutter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Machine Vision CMOS Image Sensor Market includes two main types: Rolling Shutter and Global Shutter. Rolling Shutter captures images by scanning line by line, resulting in potential distortion when capturing fast-moving objects. On the other hand, Global Shutter captures the entire image simultaneously, eliminating distortion and providing more accurate image data. Rolling Shutter sensors are cost-effective and widely used in applications with slower moving objects, while Global Shutter sensors are preferred for high-speed applications where image accuracy is crucial.</p></p>
