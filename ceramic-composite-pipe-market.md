These pipes come in different types such as centrifugal casting composite ceramic pipes, laminated wear-resistant ceramic pipes, and spot welded wear-resistant ceramic pipes. Centrifugal casting pipes are made using a casting technique, laminated pipes have multiple layers of ceramic for added strength, and spot welded pipes have ceramic tiles welded onto a metal base.
