<p><p>Food irradiation services use various types of radiation such as Electron Beam Radiation, Gamma Radiation, and X-Ray Radiation to treat food products for safety and preservation purposes. Electron Beam Radiation works by using high-energy electrons to disrupt molecular structures in food, while Gamma Radiation uses gamma rays from radioactive isotopes for the same purpose. X-Ray Radiation, on the other hand, uses X-rays to achieve similar effects. Each type of radiation offers unique benefits and applications in the food irradiation service market.</p></p>
