<p><p>Inrush current limiters (ICL) are devices used to limit the high initial current surge when electrical equipment is powered on. There are three main types of ICL markets: NTC inrush current limiters, which use negative temperature coefficient thermistors to limit current; PTC inrush current limiters, which use positive temperature coefficient thermistors; and Others market, which includes alternative technologies such as resistors or capacitors used for current limiting. Each type of ICL has its own advantages and applications in various electrical systems.</p></p>
