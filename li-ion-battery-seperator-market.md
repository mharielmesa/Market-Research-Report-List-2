<p><p>Li-ion Battery Separator Market Types include Microporous, Nonwovens, Ion-Exchange Membranes, Supported Liquid Membranes, and Other. Microporous separators are widely used in Li-ion batteries for their high porosity and ability to prevent electrical contact between anode and cathode. Nonwovens are made from synthetic fibers and offer good mechanical strength and chemical resistance. Ion-Exchange Membranes selectively allow ions to pass through. Supported Liquid Membranes employ a liquid electrolyte layer for ion transport. Other separators may include ceramic or polymer-based materials.</p></p>
