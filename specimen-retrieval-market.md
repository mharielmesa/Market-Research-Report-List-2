<p><p>Specimen retrieval devices can be categorized into detachable and non-detachable types. Detachable devices allow for easy removal of the specimen without removing the entire device from the body, providing convenience and flexibility during procedures. On the other hand, non-detachable devices are designed to secure and hold the specimen in place until it can be safely removed as a whole unit. Both types offer unique benefits depending on the specific needs of the procedure and the preferences of the healthcare provider.</p></p>
