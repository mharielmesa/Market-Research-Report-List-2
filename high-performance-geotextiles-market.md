<p><p>High Performance Geotextiles are divided into three main types: Non Woven, Woven, and Knitted. Non Woven High Performance Geotextiles are made by bonding fibers together through mechanical, thermal or chemical processes. Woven High Performance Geotextiles are created by weaving synthetic fibers together to form a durable fabric. Knitted High Performance Geotextiles are produced by interlocking loops of yarn. Each type of geotextile offers unique characteristics and advantages for various applications in industries such as construction, agriculture, and environmental protection.</p></p>
